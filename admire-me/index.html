Beyond the Keyboard

Passions, Pursuits, and Play

YouTube Creator

I craft videos driven by moments of inspiration, capturing the essence of my journey. Recently, I've been deeply immersed in a transformative self-improvement journey, and my videos are a testament to that evolution, sharing insights and inspiring others.

Fitness lifestyle

Living a fit life isn't just about the muscle gains or the calories burned—it's about feeling grounded, balanced, and genuinely alive. Lifting weights centers me, while those outdoor challenges give me a mental clarity I didn't know I was missing. And let's not forget food! Eating right has been a game-changer.

Skating

Riding my e-skate isn't just a hobby—it's freedom on wheels. Gliding around town, I've discovered nooks and crannies I never knew existed. It's not only transformed the way I travel but also how I see my surroundings. There's something truly liberating about feeling the wind rush past, taking the scenic route, and stumbling upon hidden gems right in my own town.

Dancing

Dancing for me is like speaking a universal language. Whether it's jazz, rock, or the latest pop hit, every beat calls out to me, and I can't help but respond. I'm that person who heads to the dance floor solo, not waiting for the crowd, setting the rhythm, and igniting the party spirit. Dancing isn't just movement; it's pure, uninhibited joy.

Reading

I've always found comfort in books. Over the years, I've dived into so many genres, but self-improvement ones stick out. They've shaped my thoughts, influenced decisions, and often acted as that much-needed advice. It's personal growth, one book at a time.

Gaming

A big part of my downtime and a big part of why I took the tech route in my professional career. Gaming isn't just about winding down, it's where I get to hang out with friends that live far away, and it's where I get to be a part of a community that shares my passion.
